What types of problems can be helped with counseling/psychotherapy?

Counselling is all about caring and thus the clinic offers a professional and confidential setting wherein the patients get an opportunity to discuss their problems and issues faced by them into their daily life, explore and understand them better and resolve them completely. Most problems can be helped with counselling/psychotherapy. Below is a list of some common problems for which an individual, couple, or family might seek professional help:
