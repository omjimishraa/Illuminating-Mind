How It Works

The most simple & convenient way to access talk therapy - anytime, anywhere, any device.

Choose The Right Plan

Decide on the number of sessions you would like to opt for.

Know Your Needs

Answer a few quick questions and get a therapist recommendation right away.

Get Matched

Choose the recommended therapist or talk to a matching expert who
will connect you with the right therapist based on your needs.

Schedule A Sessions

Choose a convenient time slot and get an appointment with your therapist.

Get Therapy

At the scheduled time, join the session with your therapist using the
mobile application or web browser.

Regular Messages

In addition to the scheduled video sessions, you can reach out
to your therapist via voice messages or chat. The therapist will
respond 1-2 times a day, based on availability.

Continuous Support

You have the flexibility to reschedule sessions or change
the counsellor at any point. For any issues or support,
Felicity helpdesk has you covered.
